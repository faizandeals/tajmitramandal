About Us

SARVAJANIK MOSHIYAR BABA TAJ MITRA MANDAL is a registered non-profit that operates a Masjid and Madarsa in Malwani, Mumbai. We provide free Islamic education, distribute textbooks and offer daily nutritious meals to under-privileged children and families. Your generous donations sustain these life-changing services.

Free Islamic Education

Qualified teachers deliver Qur'an and Hadith studies to 150+ students.

Free Books & Material

Every learner receives textbooks, notebooks and stationery at no cost.

Free Food Programme

Daily meals ensure no child studies on an empty stomach.
